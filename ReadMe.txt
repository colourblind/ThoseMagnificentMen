2D side-viewed multi-player single-keyboard dogfighting shenanigans!

Inspired by many happy hours playing a similar game with 4 people crowded round an Amiga.

Still very much a work in progress.

CONTROLS

Player 1 (red)
Turn anti-clockwise:    Z
Turn clockwise:         X
Fire:                   C

Player 2 (green)
Turn anti-clockwise:    I
Turn clockwise:         O
Fire:                   P
